Job Application Process

Our typical job application process.

1

Application

Submit your application online. Our AI will review your application and match you with the best-fit jobs.

2

AI Interview

Our AI will conduct an initial interview to assess your skills and qualifications.

3

Final Interview

If you pass the AI interview, you will be invited for a final interview with the hiring manager.

4

Offer

If you are selected, you will receive a job offer.
